A history for new and old generations

AYRTON SENNA Tribute 1994 / 2014 is a project in collaboration with Amedeo Abello. To celebrate the 20th anniversary of the death of the famous driver Ayrton Senna, Formula Passion (one of the most important Italian motor magazine) and Ayrton Senna Institute organized an international event in Imola (Italy). Our mission was to create an image that was able to represent his history and join new and old generations.

The Letter Logos

Our idea was to create for each letter of the name “Ayrton Senna” a letter logo. Each letter logo represents as an initial the beginning of a story to hand down from one generation to another. Inside these letter logos are the green and yellow Ayrton helmet images, universally recognized as the emblem of his epic challenges.

The Mechanical Initials

Thanks to the helmet image the initial is transformed into a mechanical component. The logo letter is an overlap of 3 different fonts of the same typeface with different perspectives of the helmet image inside. The letter logos can be used together, separately or with the acronym AS.
